About Limoo

Limoo is a curated collection of places in Stockholm, gathered by two people who simply love food and good vibes.

We often find ourselves wanting to go out but not quite knowing where. Sometimes we’re in the mood for a cozy bar, sometimes a buzzing café, and sometimes something completely different.

That’s why we created Limoo — a guide built around mood and feeling, not endless lists, ratings, or algorithms.

Every spot you find here is handpicked by us, places we genuinely like and want to share, whether simple or refined.

It’s not about covering every restaurant in the city or matching everyone’s taste, but about sharing the places that feel special to us and that we think are worth visiting. The list keeps growing as we discover new places we love.

We simply hope you enjoy these places as much as we do.
